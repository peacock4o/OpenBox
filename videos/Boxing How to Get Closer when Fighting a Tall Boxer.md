## Original description

How the shorter boxer can get into close distance when fighting a taller opponent.
Learn effective ways to get inside against a taller boxer. This video shows how to close the distance using angles, slips, and footwork, how to avoid their long-range punches, and how to land short hooks, uppercuts, and body shots safely to take control of the fight.
