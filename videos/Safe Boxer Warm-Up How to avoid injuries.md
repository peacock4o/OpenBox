## Original description

Learn why the correct boxing stance is essential not just for defense, but for saving your spine from long-term injuries. In this video, we break down the natural biomechanics of the body—explaining cervical lordosis and thoracic kyphosis—and demonstrate how "tucking up" (the proper boxing guard) naturally decompresses your back. Discover the "Owl Looks at the Moon" neck stretch, why traditional head rolls can cause spinal hernias. Train hard, but train smart!
