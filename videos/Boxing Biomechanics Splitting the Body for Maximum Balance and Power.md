## Original description

In this lesson, we break down the fundamental mechanics of the human body to improve your striking efficiency. By understanding how to "split" the body into left/right and top/bottom halves, you can master balance and coordinate the perfect kinetic chain.

We explore the crucial role of the clavicle and shoulder turnover in transforming rotational inertia into forward translational power.

Learn why the sequence of movement—from the heel to the fist—is the key to hitting harder without losing your equilibrium.

🥊 Key Training Topics:
The Axis of Rotation: Understanding the vertical and horizontal division of the body for maximum balance.

The Kinetic Chain: A step-by-step breakdown of how power travels from the floor, through the legs, into the pelvis, and out through the fist.

Clavicle & Shoulder Mechanics: Why the shoulder doesn't just rotate with the back, but moves independently via the clavicle for translational movement.

Mass Redirection: Learning to move your body weight (mass) forward without losing defensive integrity.
