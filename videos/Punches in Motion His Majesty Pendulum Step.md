## Original description

"Punches in Motion" is a professional training course focused on mastering the art of boxing footwork and striking mechanics. Learn how to generate maximum power through seamless movement, maintain perfect balance, and dominate the ring using technical shifting, angles, and the advanced Soviet pendulum step. Turn your movement into your ultimate weapon.

The course consists of six comprehensive lessons, each approximately one hour long, covering six distinct topics:

Training 1 Types of boxing footwork
Training 2 Pendulum Step
Training 3 Long distance
Training 4 Defensive actions in motion
Training 5 Attack-Provocation: Getting inside
Training 6 Counter-Punching: Sidestep
