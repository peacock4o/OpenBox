## Original description

https://inlabx.com/ 
X1pro boxing pushup stands were designed for boxers and other MMA artists.
Unstable platform, interfinger grip and pressure points for punching knuckles will help fighters to strengthen their wrists, carpal bones, wrist ligaments and muscles.
X1pro make the athlete to constantly catch his balance while doing pushups training his body and hand muscles.
As a result the fighter dramatically improves his punching power and hand rigidity.
