## Original description

This instructional video demonstrates a specialized boxing drill performed while lying on the ground, designed to build psychological resilience and technical defense. The primary goal is to desensitize the fighter to incoming punches, teaching them to keep their eyes open and maintain composure under pressure. The exercise emphasizes a "monolithic" defensive structure—engaging the neck, core, and shoulders to absorb impact without the support of the legs. It is an effective method for both children and experienced athletes to master tight grouping and shoulder rotation.
